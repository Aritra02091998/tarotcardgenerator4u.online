Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Mystic Tarot uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored in your browser when you visit websites. They help remember your preferences, personalize your session, and improve user experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use browser local storage and session-based cookies to:

  • Remember your tarot reading history during a session
  • Store your question input for context-aware readings
  • Preserve theme or deck style selections
  • Prevent repetition of the same card within a single session

3. No Tracking or Third-Party Ads

Mystic Tarot does not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or analytics tools that collect personal data. Your privacy and spiritual exploration remain uninterrupted.

4. Managing Your Preferences

You can clear your browser’s cache and local storage at any time to reset your session and erase stored history. This can also be done within our History Panel by selecting "Clear History".

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page. Continued use of the site indicates acceptance of the latest policy version.

6. Contact

For questions about this Cookie Policy, please reach out through the Contact Us section of our site.
