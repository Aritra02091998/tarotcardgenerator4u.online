Privacy Policy

At Mystic Tarot, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide while using our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect anonymous, non-personal data including session details, user interactions, and preferences. When using features such as reading history, questions are stored locally within your browser for your convenience.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used to improve your user experience, provide personalized card readings, and allow you to revisit your history. We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage in your browser to remember your session preferences and reading history. You can clear this data at any time from the history panel or through your browser settings.

4. External Services

We may use external libraries and services such as Google Fonts, Tailwind CSS, and Font Awesome. These services may collect usage statistics through their own cookies or analytics tools.

5. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information but remind users that no system is entirely immune to risks. Please avoid entering sensitive personal details.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We encourage users to review it periodically for any changes.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please reach out through the Contact Us section.
